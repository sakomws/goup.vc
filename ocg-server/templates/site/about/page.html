Our story

From speakers chat to global alliance

What started as a small TechBrains 2025 speakers chat evolved into a 450+ person alliance of engineers, founders, researchers, AI practitioners, and builders across Azerbaijan, the US, Europe, and beyond.

We still operate with the same simple principle: help useful people find each other, share what they are building, and create more value than we consume.

As long as we continue giving more than we take, the best is yet to come.

The next five years will be transformative. AI is reshaping nearly every industry, and the opportunity for serious builders has never been greater.

Who we are

A global alliance of builders

GOUP is a referral-based global alliance of builders.

We are founders, engineers, researchers, creators, community leaders, and lifelong learners from different cultures, ages, beliefs, professions, and countries.

We believe diversity of experiences creates stronger ideas, better companies, and better humans.

We are united not by geography, titles, or credentials, but by values.

A place where

People do not have to be alike

Alignment is more important than agreement.

Trust matters more than scale.

Relationships matter more than transactions.

Contribution matters more than credentials.

People just have to care.

Privacy Policy

How GOUP handles community data.

We collect only what we need to run the community, review membership, connect members, and keep the platform useful and safe.

{% endblock jumbotron -%} {% block content -%}
Last updated: June 26, 2026. This policy explains how GOUP uses information submitted through goup.vc and related community workflows.

Information we collect

We may collect account details, profile information, city or group membership requests, event participation, job or sponsorship submissions, mentorship requests, messages you submit through forms, and basic technical information such as browser, device, and security logs.

If you share links such as LinkedIn profiles, websites, social handles, company details, photos, or bios, we use them to review fit, build trust, and help other members understand who you are.

How we use information

Membership review

To review registrations, approve city/group access, and maintain a high-trust community.

Community features

To power profiles, events, jobs, mentorship, search, invitations, and member discovery.

Communication

To send confirmations, notifications, updates, and relevant operational messages.

Safety and operations

To prevent abuse, debug issues, improve reliability, and understand aggregate platform usage.

What may be visible

Some profile and community information may be visible to other members or public visitors depending on the feature. This can include your name, username, bio, photo, social links, city/group affiliation, event participation, and content you choose to publish.

Private form submissions, admin review notes, and operational logs are not intended to be public.

Sharing and service providers

We do not sell member data. We may share information with service providers that help us operate GOUP, such as hosting, email delivery, analytics, security, and automation tools. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect the community, or with your direction.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, support community operations, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security. You can ask us to update or remove information where appropriate.

Your choices

You can update your profile information from your account settings where available. You can also choose what optional information you share, such as bio, website, LinkedIn, social links, and profile photo.

To request access, correction, or deletion of your information, contact the GOUP team through the site or community administrators.

Contact

Questions about privacy or data handling can be sent to GOUP community administrators. If a dedicated privacy contact is added later, this page will be updated.
